To register a vehicle's number plate for OCR-based cardless, contactless entry:

  1. Obtain the necessary registration form from the relevant authority.
  2. Provide details of the vehicle, including the license plate number.
  3. The vehicle number is connected to your unit number for authorised entry.

Reminder: One unit only allows two car parking lots.
